About Us

We offer best holiday packages for your holiday to Sri Lanka with world class hospitality.

Welcome to Secret of Sri Lanka


At SOSL, we provide comfortable, safe, and friendly transport services for travelers exploring the beauty of Sri Lanka. Whether it’s your first visit or a return trip to this island paradise, we are here to make your journey smooth — from the moment you land at the airport to every step of your adventure.

We offer reliable airport pickups, hotel transfers, and custom transport options to popular destinations across the country. Our goal is to give tourists a personal and memorable travel experience, guided with care and genuine hospitality.

About the Owner.


Secret of Sri Lanka is proudly operated by Buddika Liyanage , a passionate and experienced tourism driver who began his journey in 2015. With years of hands-on experience serving international tourists, he understands the needs of travelers and goes the extra mile to ensure comfort, punctuality, and flexibility.

Mr. Buddika is known for his friendly attitude, safe driving, and deep knowledge of local attractions. Many of his clients return or recommend him to others — not just for the transport service, but for the kindness and professionalism he offers along the way.
